Hailstackz Productions was created in 2017 by Krystal Hailstalk. Krystal is a 26 year old videographer, born and raising in Fredericksburg, Virginia. Shortly after she moved to Austin, Texas where she is currently filming and editing music videos for upcoming artist from all over.
